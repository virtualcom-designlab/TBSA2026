# Submission Guidelines


Your submission should have been done in [EasyChair Persuasive2026](https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=persuasive2026 ) until the deadline.  
You can choose the **New Submission** tab in the website and choose TBSA2026 Track in the selection lists.

## Deadlines:
- Submission deadline: Jan. 11th, 2026 (AOE)

## Format

## Review 
- All submissions will be peer-reviewed.

## Presentation
- Accepted papers will be presented during the March 10 (Workshop Day) program.

## Publication
- All accepted papers will be published in the Springer Communications in Computer and Information Science (CCIS) series.
(CCIS proceedings)
- At least one author must register for the conference and attend the workshop in person.
- Participation in all workshops and tutorials requires registration for Persuasive Technology 2026.

## Notes
- Submissions must be original and not under review elsewhere *based on Persuasive 2026 WS policy*.
